EARLYBIRD

EarlyBird is an application made by PSHS-CVisC scholars. It's a utility software
for Moodle/KHub that automatically gets your assignments and quizzes.

THE DEVELOPERS

Meet Andry Tumacole, Basil Mendoza, and Ezra Magbanua! They are a group of grade 12 scholars that have a passion for computer science and helping their fellow scholars.

THE RESEARCH

This study’s findings can mitigate the time management problems encountered by students under an online learning setup. The application has the potential to reduce stress and anxiety, meanwhile, enhancing the learners’ productivity in online learning.
