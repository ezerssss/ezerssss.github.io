earlybird

earlybird is an application made by PSHS-CVisC scholars. It's a utility software for Moodle/KHub that automatically gets your assignments and quizzes.

Sorry! No support for mobile or for your screen size. Please contact the devolopers if you have any questions.

The Research

This study’s findings can help mitigate the time management problems encountered by students under an online learning setup. The application has the potential to reduce stress and anxiety, meanwhile, enhancing the learners’ productivity in online learning.
